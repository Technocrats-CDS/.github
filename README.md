# Technocrats — Oyo State NYSC ICT CDS Group

**Technocrats** is the Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Community Development Service (CDS) group of the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) in Oyo State, Nigeria.

We are a community of corps members who are passionate about technology and are committed to using our skills to serve and develop the state during our service year.

---

## 🌍 About Us

The Technocrats ICT CDS group brings together corps members with backgrounds in software engineering, data science, cybersecurity, product design, and other technology disciplines. We meet regularly to collaborate on projects that solve real problems in Oyo State communities.

As a CDS group under the NYSC Oyo State scheme, our activities are guided by the NYSC mandate of national unity and community development. We channel our technical expertise into building digital solutions that benefit state residents, NYSC participants, and the wider public.

> Learn more about us on our blog: [The Oyo State NYSC ICT CDS Group – Technocrats](https://oyokopa.wordpress.com/the-oyo-state-nysc-ict-cds-group-technocrats/)

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

To leverage technology for community development in Oyo State by building impactful digital solutions, fostering tech skills among corps members and host communities, and leaving a sustainable legacy beyond the service year.

---

## 🚀 Current Projects

### OYO Portal
A centralized, state-level digital platform providing official information, verified community projects, and regulated business visibility for Oyo State NYSC participants and residents.

**Key features:**
- 📢 **Announcements & News** — Verified updates from state administrators in one place
- 🏗️ **Project Showcase** — Corps members can submit and showcase their community development projects
- 🏪 **Business Directory** — A paid, time-bound listing service for local businesses seeking official visibility
- 🔐 **Admin CMS** — Role-based content management with full audit trails

**Tech stack:** Next.js · Strapi CMS · PostgreSQL · Paystack

➡️ [oyoportal-frontend](https://github.com/Technocrats-CDS/oyoportal-frontend)
